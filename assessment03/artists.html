bag raiders

[SPAIN]

Bag Radiers

Back and better than ever, Bag Raiders today deliver on another stellar release to adoring and loyal fans worldwide. With the brand new album Horizons on the way, Australia won’t have to wait long to catch the dynamite live, but for now enjoy the fresh new tune and prepare yourself for what’s to come.

image source: © wine machine 2023

caribou

[canada]

caribou

"In music I will have an idea to put some different sounds together or a melody that meshes with a chord sequence or sonic mood...I like taking mental ideas apart and playing around with them. That's what appeals to me about what I've spent my life doing"

image source: © getty images 2023

Cub Sport

[AUST]

Cub Sport

There are few trajectories in Australian music more remarkable than that of Cub Sport. Prolific and influential, they are arguably the most successful independent band in the country. Cub Sport have achieved some of the highest honours in Aussie music, placing in triple j’s Hottest 100 five times.

image source: © splendour in the grass 2023

Daft punk

[france]

Daft punk

Daft Punk are a French electronic music team who emerged from the French house music scene to become global stars in dance music. Their desire to avoid being filmed or heard speaking led them to wearing iconic robot outfits, demonstrating the blend of futuristic elements with classic disco and pop sounds.

image source: © ABC 2023

flume

[AUST]

flume

For the past decade, Flume has dominated the music world with his hypnotic and abstract compositions that awaken the senses. Born as Harley Edward Streten, the Australian producer has a fresh approach when it comes to building out an immersive soundscape that revolves around the pursuit of creative freedom.

image source: © splendour in the grass 2023

the jungle giants

[AUST]

the jungle giants

With over 150 million streams worldwide and growing, The Jungle Giants are fast becoming an unstoppable force. Regarded as Australian indie rock royalty, they were one of only four artists to have four songs feature in triple j’s Hottest 100 2018.

image source: © wine machine 2023

Lastlings

[aust]

Lastlings

Josh and Amy Dowdle are the Gold Coast duo known as Lastlings, a pair with a deep dedication to their craft that has set them far and apart across today’s musical landscape. Their latest record aims to capture emotions that feel familiar, while transporting listeners to a universe far away that feels closer to fiction than reality.

image source: © Splendour in the grass 2023

Maribou State

[UK]

Maribou State

"Electronic music for the soul."

Maribou State is Chris Davids and Liam Ivory from Hertfordshire. Liam asked Chris to play bass for his band. When they moved up north to go to university, the band fell apart and they began making electronic music in their home studio, birthing Maribou State.

image source: © Facebook

Oscar Mulero

[south africa]

Oscar Mulero

Oscar is the newest, trending artist to work with Live Nation. Unlike anything the world has ever heard before, the 23-year-old dj/songwriter has a unique sound. When asked about what inspires his artistry, Oscarl explained: “...I get my inspiration from everywhere. I’m into different types of music, cultures and lifestyles.”

image source: © wine machine 2023

Richie Hawtin

[aust]

Richie Hawtin

Australian star Richie Hawtin has been electrifying crowds worldwide with his own style of melodic and tech-driven music for more than a decade. Hawtin has grown a global following thanks to a collab with Armin van Buuren. Armed with new music, he proves why he is so alluring as a producer and DJ, and is in such high demand worldwide.

image source: © Yours & Owls 2022

Robert Hood

[UK]

Robert Hood

Australian electronic producer Robert Hood has quickly risen to the forefront of the scene with his unique take on dance music. On the touring side, The talented artist quickly took hold of the local club circuit, becoming an in-demand name across Australia with sold-out shows as well as peak time festival appearances across the country.

image source: © Facebook

Roobah Wruhme

[south africa]

Roobah Wruhme

This music industry legend, trailblazer, and currently one of the hottest DJs and producers on the underground electronic music scene, Roobah Wruhme, came into existence more than 25 years ago. After launching Relief Records, he's been a constant and revered presence in the industry since that time.

image source: © billboard 2023

Surgeon

[aust]

Surgeon

Sydney local Surgeon, is a producer and DJ that’s occupying a unique space amongst the new wave of Australian talent. On tour he’s one of the most exciting electronic acts in the country and in the studio he’s fast becoming one of Australia’s go to producers in Chicago House.

image source: © Splendour in the grass 2023

Sneaky Sound System

[aust]

Sneaky Sound System

It was back in 2004 when Miss Connie and Black Angus started making beautiful music together.Spearheaded by a spectacular salvo of chart topping hits – UFO, Pictures and I Love It – Sneaky Sound System won ARIA Awards for Breakthrough Artist and Best Dance Release, the first of 14 nominations to‐date.

image source: © NOW1 Bali

Touch Sensitive

[aust]

Touch Sensitive

Touch Sensitive is the ultimate triple-threat; as a producer, in-demand live performer and DJ, he’s a treasured slashie for artists and audiences alike. Whether it’s remixing Rufus, collaborating with Flume or touring internationally with Flight Facilities, he’s become renowned as the man with an ear for hooks and impeccable taste.

image source: © wine machine 2023
